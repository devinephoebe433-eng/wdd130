Our Rafting Adventures

Looking for a custom group package or a private tour? Contact us today to plan your perfect trip.

Wild Nile Rapid

Our most popular adrenaline-packed adventure. Conquer famous Grade 5 rapids over a full day of excitement and river swimming.

Two days of rafting with an unforgettable night of camping under the stars on a private island. Includes meals and camping gear.
